Quick Summary

  • RunTools Calc is designed to help athletes and coaches calculate splits, pace, VO₂ estimates, and training guidance.

1) Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how RunTools Calc handles information when you use our mobile application.

2) Information We Collect

The App processes values you type in such as distance, time, splits, age, and gender for calculation purposes. This information is used only to generate outputs like pace, VO₂ estimates, fitness levels, race predictions, and training targets.

Technical data

Limited technical data (such as crash logs or diagnostics) may be collected by platform services to maintain app stability and performance.

3) How We Use Information

  • To provide split, pace, VO₂, and training calculations.
  • To improve app performance and reliability.
  • To respond to user inquiries.

4) Sharing Your Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may only be shared:

  • When you choose to share results through the app.

5) Data Storage

The App may store preferences locally on your device (such as theme or language settings). You may remove this data by uninstalling the App.

6) Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for all ages.
